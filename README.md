## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

- ✅ Which songs and artists are most/least popular on Spotify?
- ✅ What audio features (energy, loudness, tempo) drive popularity?
- ✅ How has music popularity trended over the years?
- ✅ Which genres dominate — Top Hits vs All Tracks?
- ✅ What is the relationship between Energy and Loudness?
- ✅ How does Pop genre compare to other genres in popularity?

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

- **Top 10 Most Popular & Least Popular Songs** — ranked by Spotify popularity score
- **Correlation Heatmap** — audio features analysis (energy, danceability, loudness, tempo)
- **Regression Analysis** — Energy vs Loudness relationship
- **Popularity Trends Over Years** — how music streaming evolved
- **Genre Dominance Comparison** — Top Hits vs All Tracks dataset
- **Pop Genre Deep Analysis** — feature breakdown of the most streamed genre

---

## 📊 Key Findings

- 🎵 High-energy tracks show strong positive correlation with loudness
- 📈 Music popularity on Spotify has grown consistently post-2015
- 🎸 Pop genre dominates both Top Hits and overall track counts
- 🔊 Danceability and energy are the strongest predictors of popularity
- 📉 Instrumental tracks consistently score lower in popularity metrics
